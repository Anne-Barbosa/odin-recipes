Vanilla Ice Cream

Description

This easy no-churn vanilla ice cream only requires a few simple ingredients. Use an electric mixer to whip the cream until fluffy.

Fold the whipped cream into a mixture of sweetened condensed milk and vanilla extract, then freeze until firm. The result is a rich and creamy homemade dessert.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups whipping cream
  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Beat cream in a chilled glass or metal bowl with an electric mixer until stiff peaks form.
  2. Mix condensed milk and vanilla extract together in a bowl. Fold in whipped cream with a spatula until just combined. Pour into a freezer-safe container. Freeze until completely frozen, 5 to 8 hours.
